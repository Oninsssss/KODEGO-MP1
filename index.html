FAQs

Is it safe for our pets to travel?

The safest way for your pet to travel is in a carrier that has been strapped to the seat with a seatbelt or other anchor. Make sure the carrier is large enough for your dog to stand up, turn around, and lie down. You can also use a pet seatbelt, bu t these have not been proven to protect animals during a car crash.

How many pets are safe to travel in a crate ?

Only one adult cat or dog can travel in a crate. Two kittens or puppies younger than six months can travel in a crate together as long as they're similar in size and weigh less than 20 pounds (9 kg.) each. The crate must allow the kittens or puppies to move about freely.
